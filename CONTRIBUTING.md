# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in the Kinetic Intelligence Platform.

This repository exists to document platform architecture, principles, and conceptual interfaces.

---

## Contributions

We are not currently accepting external code contributions or pull requests.

This is intentional and helps ensure:
- Scientific integrity
- Safety and validation alignment
- Protection of proprietary systems

---

## Collaboration

We welcome discussions related to:
- Research collaboration
- Institutional partnerships
- Clinical or academic validation
- Strategic engineering roles

Please reach out through appropriate channels for controlled discussions.
